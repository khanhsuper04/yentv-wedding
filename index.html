Getting around the city is easy with ride-hailing apps such as Grab or Xanh SM (similar to Uber).

Ho Chi Minh City Guide

  • Places to visit: The Independence Palace, The War Remnants Museum, Book Street, Water Puppet Show, Ben Thanh Market.
  • Where to shop: The New Playground, Thảo Điền Ward, Shopping malls (Takashimaya, Vincom Đồng Khởi, Thisky Hall,...).
  • Must-try dishes: Bánh mì Huỳnh Hoa, Ba Ghiền Broken Rice, Như 6c, Bún bò Huế Đông Ba, Phở Hòa Pasteur, Bụi Garden Restaurant, Đu Đủ Xanh restaurant (vegetarian), Pizza 4P.
  • The coffee culture: The Running Bean, Katinat, Thái Yên coffee, Cà phê trứng 3T (Egg Coffee 3T), Cộng Coffee, Rang Rang Coffee – Multiple branches across HCMC.
